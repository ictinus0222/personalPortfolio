About

I help ideas turn into real, usable products—fast.

Rooted in community and driven by curiosity, I've grown from building solo projects to leading Hackathon teams and collaborating across disciplines. I dive deep into understanding the problem, research what's out there, and plan smart—so what we build isn't just functional, but meaningful.

As a product-focused engineer, I:

  • Research and validate ideas before writing a single line of code
  • Create a "Plan of Action" of the whole design and development process
  • Collaborate with designers and engineers—or lead the entire product effort
  • Refine post-launch through feedback loops, performance tweaks, and UX polishing
